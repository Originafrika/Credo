PROBLEM

80% of Togo's economy is informal. 8M adults are invisible to credit.

Banks and MFIs reject them not because they're insolvent - but because evaluating informal profiles is manual, expensive, and inconsistent.

No payslip

No bank statement. No credit history. Rejected by design.

Days wasted

Queueing at multiple agencies to be turned away.

10.9% NPL

MFIs lack standardized scoring. Bad loans stack up.

02

IMPACT

Three consequences of credit exclusion.

Borrower

No way to know eligibility before visiting. Humiliation, wasted time, lost opportunity.

MFI

Resources wasted screening unqualified applicants. Cannot assess informal income.

Economy

Informal sector disconnected from formal finance. Growth capped for 80% of workers.

SOLUTION

Credo indexes what makes a borrower creditworthy.

User pays via Mobile Money, answers adaptive questions, uploads optional documents. AI analyses profile against partner MFI criteria - returns score, max amount, recommended institution.
